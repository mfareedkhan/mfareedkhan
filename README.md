# Hi, I'm Muhammad Fareed 👋

**MS Data Science (4.0/4.0) · Vision-Language Models, Multimodal AI & Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning · Medical AI focus · Aspiring PhD in AI/ML**

I work on making large models efficient enough to deploy under real-world constraints: small enough for modest hardware, private enough to keep sensitive data in-house.

I recently completed an MS in Data Science at Riphah International University (4.0/4.0, first in my cohort). My research sits at the intersection of vision–language models and NLP, using parameter-efficient fine-tuning (QLoRA/LoRA) and quantization to make large models practical to deploy. One focus is medical AI, for example, vision–language models that draft radiology reports from chest X-rays, but the same methods carry across domains: I've also built efficient models for low-resource languages, text summarization, and multilingual sentiment analysis.

I care as much about shipping the system as writing the paper. I'm comfortable across the full stack, from data preprocessing and model development in PyTorch/Hugging Face to fine-tuning, quantization, and evaluation.

Alongside this, I've taught mathematics and science for 8+ years, including a permanent, competitive-exam civil-service post I held right through my MS and research.
